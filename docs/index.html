Hello, my name is Marijan!
I'm an independent consultant for Software Engineering and DevOps, who lays great emphasis on software design, and reproducible and regression-free software development. I work with functional languages like Haskell, Nix/NixOS, and PureScript. Previously I used to work with modern C++.

I was born and raised in Germany, graduated in Informatics at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology and currently live on the Dalmatian coast in Croatia.

You can contact me in various languages: English (professional), German (native), Croatian (native), Spanish (fundamentals)
