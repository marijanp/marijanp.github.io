Hello, my name is Marijan!

I'm an independent consultant for Software Engineering and DevOps, focusing on robust software design and reproducible, regression-free software development. My experience spans application and backend development, as well as DevOps, across domains such as Fintech, Robotics, and Deep Learning. I also teach Nix/NixOS for Nixcademy.

I work with functional languages such as Haskell, OCaml and Nix/NixOS, alongside Rust and modern C++.

I was born and raised in Germany, graduated in Informatics at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, and live in Croatia on the Dalmatian coast.

I speak English (professional), German (native), Croatian (native), Spanish (B2).
